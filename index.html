SAMSUNG
Sketch Keypoint Detection

Identifying and extracting segmentation map and keypoints from a sketch, drawing or a painting containing humanoid figure. The primary key points of interest are all the body joints: Neck, Left and right shoulders, elbow, wrist, hip, knee, ankle.
Once these keypoints are extracted along with the segmentation map of humanoid figure, we can create wide variety of animation feeding sequences of action along with the keypoints.
The resulting network should extract all the different possible keypoints from the drawing under varying conditions like backgrounds, scales, viewpoints, etc.
